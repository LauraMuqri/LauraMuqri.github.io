# Mathematical Physics 

**PHYS-252 · Spring 2026**
---

## Course Overview

This course introduces the essential mathematical tools required for the formulation and solution of physical problems. Emphasis is placed on complex numbers, linear algebra, Fourier analysis, and special functions commonly used in classical and modern physics.
