# Mathematical Physics I

**PHYS-254 · Spring 2026**
---

## Course Overview

This course introduces core mathematical tools used in renewable energy physics, including series methods, complex analysis, linear algebra, multivariable integration, and differential equations, with applications to energy systems, materials, and power modeling.
